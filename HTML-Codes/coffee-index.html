How to Brew the Perfect Coffee

  1. Grind your coffee beans to a fine consistency.
  2. Heat water to a temperature of 195-205°F (90-96°C).
  3. Add the coffee grounds to the filter.
  4. Pour the hot water over the grounds.
  5. Let the coffee drip into your cup.

History of Coffee

Coffee has a rich history that dates back centuries. It is believed to have originated in Ethiopia, where legend has it that a goat herder named Kaldi discovered the energizing effects of coffee berries. The drink eventually made its way to the Arabian Peninsula, where it became a popular beverage among traders and travelers. In the 17th century, coffeehouses emerged in Europe, serving as hubs for intellectual discourse and social gatherings. Today, coffee is enjoyed worldwide, with various brewing methods and flavors that cater to every palate.
